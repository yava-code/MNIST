What's This Project?

Hey! So this is my take on the classic MNIST problem - basically training a neural network to recognize handwritten digits. It's like teaching a computer to read numbers the way humans do!

The MNIST dataset has 70,000 images of handwritten digits (0-9), and I've trained a model to classify them with pretty good accuracy. It's the perfect starter project for anyone getting into machine learning and computer vision.
